Viking Warrior

The Viking Warrior is a fearless berserker, charging into battle with unmatched fury. Clad in thick furs and iron-plated armor, he wields a massive two-handed axe capable of cleaving through enemies in a single strike. His raw strength allows him to break enemy lines, unleashing a devastating Whirlwind attack that sends foes flying in all directions.

Fueled by unrelenting rage, the Viking Warrior thrives in combat, growing stronger with every hit he takes.

  • Berserker Charge:
    • Allows him to rush forward with unstoppable force, knocking down anyone in his path.
  • Battle Frenzy:
    • When surrounded, reducing incoming damage and increasing his attack speed, turning him into a whirlwind of destruction.
  • Ragnarok's Wrath:
    • Legends speak of Viking warriors who, in their final moments, unleash their most powerful ability entering a trance-like state where pain no longer affects them, and every strike becomes fatal.

Those who face a Viking Warrior on the battlefield know that retreat is the only option, for he fights not for survival, but for glory.
